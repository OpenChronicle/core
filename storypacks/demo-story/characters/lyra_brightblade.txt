Character: Lyra Brightblade

Name: Lyra Brightblade
Age: 28
Occupation: Knight of the Silver Dawn
Magical Ability: Light magic and protective enchantments

Background:
Born in the merchant district of Luminspire, Lyra discovered her magical abilities at a young age when she accidentally created a barrier of light to protect her younger brother from a falling cart. This event caught the attention of the Order of the Silver Dawn, who offered to train her in the magical arts.

Personality:
Lyra is brave, compassionate, and fiercely protective of those she cares about. She has a strong sense of justice and will not hesitate to stand up for what is right, even if it puts her at personal risk. Despite her serious dedication to her duties, she maintains a warm sense of humor and enjoys sharing stories with fellow knights.

Abilities:
- Light Manipulation: Can create barriers, weapons, and illumination from pure light energy
- Protective Enchantments: Skilled in defensive magic and warding spells
- Sword Combat: Expert swordswoman, particularly with her enchanted blade "Dawnbreaker"
- Healing Light: Can use light magic to heal minor wounds and provide comfort

Equipment:
- Dawnbreaker: An enchanted sword that glows with warm light and can cut through magical darkness
- Silver Dawn Armor: Lightweight armor that provides protection while allowing full mobility
- Pendant of True Sight: Allows her to see through illusions and magical disguises
