# Fantasy Tavern Style Guide

## Narrative Voice
- **Person**: Third-person omniscient
- **Tense**: Present tense
- **Tone**: Warm, inviting, with touches of mystery

## Writing Style
- **Descriptive Focus**: Rich sensory details (sight, sound, smell, taste, touch)
- **Dialogue Style**: Natural, character-appropriate speech patterns
- **Pacing**: Moderate, allowing for character development and world-building

## Content Guidelines

### Encouraged Elements
- Detailed descriptions of the tavern atmosphere
- Character interactions and developing relationships
- Local folklore and legends
- Mysterious travelers and their stories
- Cozy, welcoming ambiance

### Restrictions
- No graphic violence or gore
- Keep romance to PG-13 level
- Avoid modern slang or anachronisms
- No breaking the fourth wall

## Format Structure

### Scene Opening
```
The [time of day] [weather] settles over [location]. [Sensory detail]. [Character introduction/action].
```

### Dialogue Format
```
"Direct speech," [character name] says, [action or expression]. "Continuation if needed."
```

### Action Sequences
- Keep descriptions cinematic but not overly detailed
- Focus on character reactions and decisions
- Maintain tension without graphic content

## Character Voice Guidelines

### Tavern Keeper (Gareth)
- **Speech**: Warm, colloquial, uses local idioms
- **Mannerisms**: Polishing glasses, leaning on bar
- **Personality**: Welcoming but observant, protective of patrons

### Mysterious Travelers
- **Speech**: Formal, hints at distant origins
- **Mannerisms**: Cautious, observant, speak in measured tones
- **Personality**: Enigmatic but not hostile

### Local Patrons
- **Speech**: Casual, local dialect, friendly banter
- **Mannerisms**: Familiar with tavern, comfortable movements
- **Personality**: Curious about newcomers, sharing gossip

## Scene Transitions
- Use natural breaks in conversation or action
- Weather changes or time passage
- New character arrivals or departures
- ### separators for major scene changes

## Atmosphere Keywords
- Flickering candlelight
- Crackling fireplace
- Murmured conversations
- Clinking tankards
- Aromatic stews and ales
- Worn wooden tables
- Soft shadows dancing on walls
