# The Wanderer's Rest - Tavern and Inn

## Physical Description

The Wanderer's Rest stands as a beacon of warmth and hospitality in the village of Millbrook. Built from sturdy oak timber and local stone, the two-story building has weathered countless seasons while maintaining its welcoming charm.

### Exterior
- **Structure**: Timber frame with stone foundation, thatched roof
- **Features**: Large oak door with iron hinges, diamond-paned windows
- **Signage**: Carved wooden sign depicting a traveler's pack and walking stick
- **Atmosphere**: Warm light spills from windows, smoke rises from chimney

### Main Tavern Room (Ground Floor)
- **Size**: Large common room accommodating 40-50 patrons
- **Layout**: Central hearth with surrounding tables and benches
- **Bar**: Solid oak counter along the far wall, lined with stools
- **Ambiance**: Flickering candlelight, crackling fire, murmured conversations
- **Decor**: Mounted deer heads, local artwork, maps of trade routes

### Private Dining Area
- **Location**: Side alcove off main room
- **Purpose**: Discrete conversations, important meetings
- **Features**: Heavy curtains for privacy, single large table
- **Clientele**: Merchants, local officials, mysterious travelers

### Kitchen
- **Location**: Behind the main bar
- **Equipment**: Large stone hearth, copper pots, spice racks
- **Specialties**: Hearty stews, fresh bread, local fish
- **Staff**: Gareth cooks most meals, assisted by local girl (Mira)

## Upper Floor (Inn Rooms)

### Room Types
- **Common Room**: 8 beds, shared space, basic amenities
- **Private Chambers**: 4 individual rooms with single beds
- **Merchant Suite**: 2 rooms, sitting area, storage for goods
- **Mysterious Guest Room**: Best room, rarely used, well-furnished

### Amenities
- **Furnishings**: Simple but comfortable, handcrafted furniture
- **Lighting**: Candles and oil lamps
- **Comfort**: Wool blankets, straw mattresses, washbasins
- **Security**: Sturdy locks, Gareth holds master keys

## Special Features

### The Hidden Room
- **Location**: Behind false wall in basement
- **Purpose**: Safe house for refugees and those in danger
- **Access**: Known only to Gareth and trusted allies
- **Contents**: Basic supplies, emergency food, hidden exit tunnel

### The Hearth Tales Corner
- **Location**: Chairs arranged around main fireplace
- **Tradition**: Evening storytelling and music
- **Participants**: Traveling bards, local elders, mysterious guests
- **Atmosphere**: Intimate gathering, shared stories, community bonding

## Services and Offerings

### Food and Drink
- **Ales**: Local brew (Millbrook Amber), imported ales
- **Spirits**: Whiskey, brandy, local fruit wines
- **Meals**: Stews, roasted meats, fresh bread, seasonal vegetables
- **Specialties**: Gareth's famous meat pies, honey cakes

### Lodging
- **Rates**: Copper for common room, silver for private chambers
- **Meals**: Included with room, served at fixed times
- **Stabling**: Available for horses and pack animals
- **Security**: Gareth keeps watch, trusted with valuables

### Information Hub
- **Trade Routes**: Current conditions, safe passages, hazards
- **Local News**: Village events, political changes, weather
- **Travelers' Tales**: Stories from distant lands, warnings, opportunities
- **Message Board**: Community announcements, job postings

## Daily Rhythm

### Morning (Dawn - Noon)
- Breakfast service for inn guests
- Local farmers and craftsmen stop by
- Preparation for evening crowd
- Deliveries and supply management

### Afternoon (Noon - Evening)
- Light lunch service
- Traveling merchants arrive
- Business discussions and deals
- Maintenance and cleaning

### Evening (Dusk - Late Night)
- Peak tavern hours
- Hearty meals and flowing ale
- Storytelling and music
- Community gathering and news exchange

### Night (Late Night - Dawn)
- Quiet hours for serious discussions
- Mysterious guests may arrive
- Gareth's final rounds
- Secure premises for the night
